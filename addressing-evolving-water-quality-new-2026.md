### Why Your Water Quality Changes
1.  **Groundwater Shifts:** As infrastructure ages or environmental factors change, the balance of minerals and substances in local aquifers can fluctuate significantly.
2.  **Emerging Contaminants:** Chemicals like PFAS are increasingly detected due to enhanced testing sensitivity, meaning we are finding things that were previously going unnoticed.
3.  **Treatment Lag:** When municipalities are in the process of upgrading to GAC or other advanced technologies, there is a period where local water may not reach the desired quality standard for every individual household.
